Abstract

The general objective of this paper is to analyse how the aerodynamics of a helicopter works.
Fistly, we are going to describe how a helicopter flies in a point of view more practical, with help of the "Basic helicopter aerodynamics".
Secondly, we are going to talk about the physics and mathematics behind this motion withthe help of the book "Blade elements - momentum aerodyamics model of a helicopter rotor operating at low-Reynolds number in ground effect".
Finally, we will analyse at a professional level using the "NDARC which stands for NASA Design and Analysis of Rotorcraft".
These are reports completed by NASA in which they combine manuals and technical worksheets.
